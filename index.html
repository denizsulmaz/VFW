VFW is excited to announce an open call for activist artists to participate in our upcoming digital NFT exhibition. As an NGO committed to promoting human rights and gender equality based on Intellectual activism, we believe that art has the power to inspire social change and raise awareness. We are seeking artists who are passionate about using their art to make a difference and who are willing to share their work with a global audience. If you are interested in being a part of this exciting exhibition, we encourage you to submit your work for consideration.
