#WhoWeAre

V For Woman is an Istanbul-based social enterprise founded in 2022 to develop equality and diversity globally, especially in Turkey. Our vision is to eliminate illegal discrimination, starting by protecting the human rights of all segments and expanding our network with representatives from many countries. We aim to break the silence by addressing a large mass. Our vision is to create safe communities and respectful relationships through awareness activities, initiatives, and collaborations in the light of science, technology, and art. We are trying to end sexual assault, domestic violence, dating violence, sexual abuse, and all kinds of sexual violence and to create awareness on this issue.
